About the Championship

The Biggest Week in Pickleball

The Carvana PPA Tour is thrilled to bring the inaugural Veolia Pickleball National Championships to Cary, North Carolina. This PPA Tour Slam offers the largest prize money and ranking points in professional pickleball, featuring the very best athletes in the sport.

Expect world No. 1's Anna Leigh Waters, Ben Johns, Gabe Tardio, and Chris Haworth alongside North Carolina residents Kate Fahey, Brooke Buckner, and more top-ranked professionals competing for titles and crucial world ranking points.

New for 2026: live music concerts, sponsor activations, renowned food and fare, pro-led pickleball camps and clinics, plus much more for the entire family.
